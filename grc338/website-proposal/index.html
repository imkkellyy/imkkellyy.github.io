Introduction

Silver Pearl

Silver Pearl is a jewelry store that opened in San Francisco. It is a small business that focuses on selling jewelry such as rings, bracelets, and necklaces. We want all our clients to have high quality pieces that they can express themselves with at affordable prices.

Target audience

Customers interested in buying jewelry either for themselves or for someone they love.

Customers should look around the website to find their most wanted jewelry. We want customers to have an awesome online shopping experience.
